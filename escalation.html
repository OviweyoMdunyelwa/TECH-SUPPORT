Tech Support Escalation Procedure

If your issue has not been resolved through our standard support channels, please follow the escalation procedure outlined below. Each level of escalation ensures that your issue receives increased attention and resources for a swift resolution.

Level 1: Initial Support

Start by contacting our Customer Support team via our standard support channels. At this stage, a support agent will work with you to troubleshoot and resolve your issue. Please provide detailed information to ensure quick and accurate assistance.

Contact: support@company.com

Phone: 1-800-555-0101

Response Time: 24 hours

Level 2: Advanced Support

If the issue remains unresolved after initial support, request an escalation to Advanced Support. At this level, a senior technician will review your case and work with the support agent to identify more advanced solutions.

Contact: advanced-support@company.com

Response Time: 12 hours

Level 3: Technical Specialist Team

If the issue persists, it will be escalated to our Technical Specialist Team. A specialist with in-depth expertise will be assigned to handle your case and may reach out for additional information or to perform remote troubleshooting if needed.

Contact: specialist@company.com

Response Time: 8 hours

Level 4: Management Review

If all previous attempts have not resolved the issue, you may request a Management Review. At this stage, our Support Management Team will review the case and coordinate resources for a priority resolution.

Contact: management-review@company.com

Response Time: 4 hours

Level 5: Executive Support

For urgent, high-impact issues affecting business continuity that cannot be resolved through the previous levels, please escalate to Executive Support. A member of our executive team will work directly with you to ensure a rapid and final resolution.

Contact: executive-support@company.com

Response Time: 1-2 hours
